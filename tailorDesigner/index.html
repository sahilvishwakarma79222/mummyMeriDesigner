Meet Mansha Vishwakarma

Master Tailor & Founder

With over a decade of experience in ladies tailoring, Mansha Vishwakarma has perfected the art of creating garments that fit flawlessly. Her journey began with stitching clothes for neighbors, and through word-of-mouth recommendations, she has become one of Ambernath's most trusted tailors.

Specializing in designer blouses and intricate embroidery work, Mansha combines traditional techniques with modern designs to create stunning outfits for all occasions. Her attention to detail and commitment to perfect fitting has earned her a loyal customer base across Ambernath.
